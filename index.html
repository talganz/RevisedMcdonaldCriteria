MS Diagnostic Assistant (2024 Revisions)

1. Patient Profile

Pediatric Alert: MOG-IgG testing is mandatory for children < 12y or ADEM presentations. CVS is positive only if ≥ 50% of lesions are involved.
Diagnostic Caution: In patients ≥ 50y or with vascular risk, additional confirmation (Spinal MRI, CSF, or CVS) is strongly recommended.

Supportive Biomarkers:
CSF Positive (OCB/kFLC)Demonstrated intrathecal IgG synthesis: OCB or positive Kappa Free Light Chain (kFLC) index are interchangeable.
CVS PositiveSelect 6 Rule: ≥6 CVS+ lesions; or majority if <10 lesions exist.
Pediatrics: Positive only if ≥50% of lesions show CVS.
DIT PositiveNew T2 lesion on follow-up, or simultaneous Gad+ and Gad- lesions on a single scan.
PRL PositiveParamagnetic Rim Lesions: Presence of ≥1 chronic active lesion with a characteristic iron rim.
