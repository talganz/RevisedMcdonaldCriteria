MS Diagnostic Assistant (2024 Revisions)

1. Patient Profile

Pediatric Tip: In patients < 12y, serum MOG-IgG is strongly recommended. For < 18y, CVS is positive only if ≥ 50% of lesions show the sign.
Vascular Risk / Age 50+: Misdiagnosis is common. Specific confirmation via Spinal MRI, CSF, or CVS is required to support diagnosis.
